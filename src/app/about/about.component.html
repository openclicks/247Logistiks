How we do it

We take a complete logistics management view to help you with:
  • Freight forwarding using ground freight
  • Customs brokering and customs clearance
  • Delivery anywhere in the world
  • Safe, reliable and cost-effective transportation of your goods
Our in-house logistics team is known for their expertise, longevity in the market, and a reputation built on good customer communication and regular reporting. We have experience in planning and know how to avoid bottlenecks to transport cargo with the necessary permits and escorts across multiple countries and jurisdictions. We specialise in the transport of mining equipment but are also established in industrial, medical, shipping, resource and general cargo.
